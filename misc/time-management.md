## Time Management
- Story Points
- We can assign a value to each requirement according to how much time/difficulty it will be
- Example:
    - step05 - 2
    - step10 - 3
    - step20 - 3
    - step30 - 4
    - step40 - 3
- Break Down
    - Hour 1 - step05
    - Hour 2 - step10, step20
    - Hour 3 - step30, step40
    - Hour 4 - catchup/buffer time
